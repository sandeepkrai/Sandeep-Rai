Experience
Internship ,Volunteership and Hackathons
I have worked with many projects as a Full stack Developer.
eSamudaay Hackathon :-
I was part of the winning team at the eSamudaay Hackathon! Our team was tasked with building a platform for food vendors to sell their products based on ONDC and we rose to the challenge by creating an innovative solution that impressed the judges. Our hard work and dedication paid off as we were awarded the first prize, which included a cash prize of 25,000 and various other goodies. This win is a testament to my technical skills, creativity, and ability to work well in a team. I am grateful for this recognition and eager to apply the lessons I learned at this hackathon to future projects.

BITS Pilani Hackathon :-
I was part of the team that won recognition at the BITS Pilani Hackathon! The challenge was to showcase the use of public APIs and our team developed a weather app using Flutter that was both functional and visually appealing. Our submission was ranked among the top 10 and we were awarded Postman goodies as a token of appreciation for our hard work and creativity. This experience allowed me to demonstrate my technical abilities and showcased my ability to work effectively in a team. I am grateful for this opportunity and excited to build upon my success at this hackathon in the future.
Accelth :-
I had the opportunity to volunteer for Accelth organization and contribute to their digital presence by developing a Flutter application. With my expertise in mobile app development, I was able to create an intuitive and user-friendly app that met the organization's needs. The application is now available on the Google Play Store for users to download and enjoy. I am proud to have made a positive impact on Accelth's mission and grateful for this rewarding experience.

Dronaid :-
I had the privilege of volunteering for Dronaid, a student project at MIT Manipal, where I developed a Flutter application to revolutionize the way people order their medicines. The app I created allowed users to easily place an order for their needed medicines with just a few taps, making the process fast, simple, and convenient. Working on this project gave me the chance to apply my skills and make a difference in people's lives. I am proud to have contributed to this innovative project and excited to see the positive impact it will have in the future.
